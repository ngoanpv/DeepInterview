# ExampleCorp — Senior Backend Engineer (FICTIONAL sample)

> This is a fictional example playbook demonstrating the skill format. It contains no real
> company facts. Real playbooks are distilled from interview runs and pass a review gate (WP-10).

## Round structure
1. Intro + experience walkthrough (15m)
2. System design — design a rate limiter (45m)
3. Behavioral — conflict + ownership (30m)

## Question bank
- "Walk me through a system you scaled and the bottleneck you hit."
- "Design a multi-region rate limiter. What are the consistency trade-offs?"

## Signals
- Quantifies impact; reasons about trade-offs explicitly; handles ambiguity.

## Pitfalls
- Jumps to implementation before clarifying requirements/constraints.
